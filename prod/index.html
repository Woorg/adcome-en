Building effective
email marketing in 4 steps

  • Detailed analysis
    We dive into the specifics of your business: we learn about your product, clients purchasing power, your current sales pipeline and the entire customer journey from the first connection to the purchase
  • Developing a detailed email marketing strategy
    We will identify the target audience and break it down into segments that will receive personalized emails at the right time. We will create a content plan that will consist of regular and trigger emails, which will allow you to always stay in touch with customers and motivate them to buy
  • Implementing the developed strategy
    We will develop email templates, create an attractive design in your corporate style and lay out ready-to-send letters, install subscription forms on the site, prepare a content plan, and integrate game mechanics
  • Analysis of results
    We will analyze the results and prepare reports within each project for every reporting period

Our case studies

Bekker

An international company providing a wide range of garden goods. The main products are seeds and seedlings of various garden crops

What was done

A gamification marathon was launched and it consisted of three games. Each game lasted for a week

The result of our work

  • First game: during the promotion, the transaction rate increased by 78%, and the average daily revenue increased by 43% compared to the same period.
  • Second game: the transaction rate over the period of the promotion increased by 42% compared to the same period last year, and income by 64%.
  • Third game: the transaction rate over the period of the promotion increased by 35% compared to the same period last year, and income by 40%.
